# Privacy Policy for Math Stickman Challenge

**Effective Date:** [Current Date]

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Math Stickman Challenge**. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you play our mobile game.

## 2. Information We Collect
We intentionally collect as little personal information as possible to provide a seamless and secure gaming experience:
*   **Account Information:** When you register, we collect a username and a password. We **do not** collect your email address, real name, phone number, or physical address.
*   **Game Data:** We collect information about your gameplay, such as your high scores, questions answered correctly/incorrectly, and your in-game inventory (coins earned, power-ups, and accessories).
*   **Security Data:** To secure your account and allow for password resets, we generate a random "Recovery Phrase". We only store a secure cryptographic hash of this phrase in our database, never the raw text.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We strictly use your information to operate the game:
*   To create, authenticate, and maintain your account.
*   To save your game progress across sessions.
*   To manage your in-game currency and items.
*   To facilitate secure account recovery if you forget your password.

## 4. Data Storage and Security
Your data is securely stored using Supabase, our backend service provider. We utilize industry-standard security practices:
*   Passwords and recovery phrases are cryptographically hashed (SHA-256 or bcrypt) before storage. They cannot be read in plain text by anyone.
*   We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure that only you can access your own game progression data.

## 5. Sharing of Information
We highly value your privacy and **do not** sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. We may share data only with:
*   **Service Providers:** We use Supabase to host our secure database. They act as a data processor and are bound by strict data protection agreements.
*   **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, court order, or governmental regulation, we may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Math Stickman Challenge is a math-focused educational game. While suitable for children, we do not require or knowingly collect personally identifiable information (like emails or real names) from users, including children under 13. All accounts are anonymous usernames. If you believe we have inadvertently collected restricted personal information from a child, please contact us so we can immediately delete it.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. We advise you to review this page occasionally for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.
