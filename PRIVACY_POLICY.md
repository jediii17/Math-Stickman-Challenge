# Privacy Policy for Math Stickman Challenge

**Effective Date:** February 27, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Math Stickman Challenge**. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you play our mobile game, in strict compliance with the **Google Play Families Policies**.

## 2. Information We Collect
We intentionally collect as little personal information as possible to provide a seamless and secure gaming experience. **We do not collect any personal or sensitive information from children.**

*   **Account Information:** When you register, we collect an anonymous username and a password. We **do not** collect your email address, real name, phone number, physical address, or device phone number.
*   **Game Data:** We collect information about your gameplay (high scores, questions answered, coins earned, power-ups, and accessories) to save your progress.
*   **Restricted Data:** We **do not** request or collect Precise Location, Microphone data, Camera sensor data, or Bluetooth data. 
*   **Security Data:** To secure your account, we generate a random "Recovery Phrase". We only store a secure cryptographic hash of this phrase, never the raw text.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We strictly use your information to operate the game:
*   To create, authenticate, and maintain your account.
*   To save your game progress across sessions.
*   To manage your in-game currency and items.

## 4. Advertising & Monetization
We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements within the app. Because Math Stickman Challenge is designed for families:
*   We exclusively use **Google Play Families Self-Certified Ads SDKs**.
*   We **do not** display interest-based (personalized) advertising or remarketing to our users.
*   We **do not** transmit the Android Advertising Identifier (AAID), SIM Serial, Build Serial, BSSID, MAC, SSID, IMEI, or IMSI to our ad partners.
*   All ads are screened to be globally appropriate for children and families.

## 5. Social Features
Math Stickman Challenge is a safe, single-player experience. The app **does not** contain any social networking features, chat rooms, or capabilities for users to exchange freeform media or personal information with strangers.

## 6. Data Storage and Security
Your data is securely stored using Supabase, our backend service provider. We utilize industry-standard security practices:
*   Passwords and recovery phrases are cryptographically hashed (SHA-256 or bcrypt).
*   We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure that only you can access your own progression data.

## 7. Legal Compliance (COPPA & GDPR)
We ensure that our app and all integrated third-party services (Supabase, AdMob) are fully compliant with the **U.S. Children's Online Privacy and Protection Act (COPPA)**, the **E.U. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, and other applicable global privacy laws. If you believe we have inadvertently collected restricted personal information from a child, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in legal or regulatory requirements. We advise you to review this page occasionally.
